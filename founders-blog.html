Nothing Needs You Right Now

January 19, 2026

People think pressure comes from problems. It doesn’t.

Pressure comes from continuity.
From carrying thoughts longer than they need to exist.

Most thoughts are temporary guests that never learned how to leave.

They knock.
You answer.
Then you offer them a chair, a drink, a future.

No one told you that was optional.

— Kentaro

Doing less on purpose

January 14, 2026

I kept looking for an app that would help me “deal with my thoughts,” but most tools felt like another job: track this, reflect on that, improve something, stay consistent.

I wanted the opposite — something that asks almost nothing.

Think Notepad or TextEdit, which I used to dump my thoughts (mostly tasks), and that still helps the mind feel a little cleaner.

MINDBEBOP is my attempt at that: a few ultralight actions that reduce thought residue without turning life into a project.

— Kentaro
